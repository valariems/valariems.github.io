measuring usability with SUS scoring

One of the first projects I tackled when I joined OrgSync in 2012 was a redesign of the organization listings at a university. The utilitarian page design looked more like a grueling spreadsheet than an inviting showcase of campus clubs.

Project Goals

Design Refresh

Appeal to our audience of college students accustomed to seeing better information presentation on other sites they frequented.

Better Information

Give students more information about an organization that might entice them to become involved on campus.

Improved Filtering

Wireframe Exploration and Interation

After thorough research of other sites used by our audience, I made the decision to feature organization profile photos. Hover cards would allow additional information to be presented about an organization—like its next upcoming event—without cluttering or distracting from the primary page information.
