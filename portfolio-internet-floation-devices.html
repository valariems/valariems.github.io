Site Design

Web design and social media firm

This Drupal web design and social media shop wanted a marketing website that “doesn’t look like a Drupal site.” The site needed to be built in Drupal, using existing Drupal core and contributed modules, with no custom developer code.

My role

I created the site concept and design, build the site in the Drupal CMS, and created a custom subtheme from the Fusion base theme—all within a two-week time period.

Successes

Immediately after site launch, the company became a partner with Acquia, the #1 software vendor on the 2012 Inc. 500 list. This site demonstrated that a Drupal site could have a sleek, unique look and feel, which was fairly unusual at that time in the Drupal community.
