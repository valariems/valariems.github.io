I'm passionate about designing great user interfaces.
They begin as a puzzle. Identify the problem, then seek a solution. Build this page, this workflow, this series of tasks, so the user's interaction is so seamless that they don't even think. I put years of layout and typography experience, along with a healthy dose of user empathy, into every design I create.
My career began over 20 years ago as a print production artist and designer for a retail apparel company in St. Louis. After a move to Dallas in 1994, I began my freelance career that spanned several years, working with major corporations and agencies throughout the area.
Today, I harness the power
of CSS, HTML, page layout, typography, and years of experience to create interfaces that enhance business and community. I've always believed that form follows function, and that user experience and content drives design. Because when it works for your user, it works for you.
