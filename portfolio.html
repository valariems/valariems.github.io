Snapshots: Interface and graphic design

While my passion is the user experience, my visual design skills and background in typography, page layout, and color theory enables me to bring a holistic, multidisciplinary approach to user experience design.

Process exploration: Web application feature

While at OrgSync during 2014-2015, I redesigned a complex process used by universities to review and approve events run by student organizations. From initial exploration of the problem and goals, through prototypes to hand off to development, this feature upgrade was widely regarded as one of the top successes that the platform had seen.

Wireframe exploration: Web application feature

While at OrgSync in 2013, I redesigned one of the web application's primary features: the browse organization tool. University students visit this page within their campus community to find student organizations that may interest them, or they may already belong to.
